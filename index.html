About

Welcome to CS Puzzles & Games! This collection of interactive puzzles, games, and educational concepts is designed to help students learn computer science in a fun and engaging way. Each section focuses on different aspects of computer science, from fundamental concepts to complex problem-solving scenarios.

Feel free to explore the concepts, puzzles, and games, and don't forget to challenge yourself with the more difficult ones!
